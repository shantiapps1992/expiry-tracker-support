Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive notifications?

Scannly sends grouped notifications at 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before expiry, and on the expiry day. If multiple items expire on the same day, you'll get one notification showing all items. Set your preferred notification time in Settings.

Notification time setting is disabled?

The notification time picker is controlled by iOS notification permissions. Enable notifications in iOS Settings ‚Üí Scannly ‚Üí Notifications first, then you can set your preferred time in the app.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Your data is stored locally on your device for privacy. Scannly does not upload your data to any server. You have full control with manual export/import options.

What products can I track?

Any product with an expiry date: food items, medicines, vitamins, supplements, cosmetics, skincare products, baby formula, packaged goods, and more.

How does OCR scanning work?

Point your camera at the expiry date text, use zoom controls for curved bottles or small text, and the app will detect and extract the date. Supports various date formats.

Can I import data from other devices?

Yes! Export your data on the old device, save the backup file to cloud storage (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.), then import it on your new device. Duplicates are automatically skipped.
