Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my inventory?

You can add items in three ways: manually enter details, scan product barcodes, or use the camera to automatically detect expiry dates using OCR technology.

When will I receive notifications?

You'll receive notifications at 7 days, 3 days, 1 day, and on the expiry day. You can customize the notification time in Settings.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy. You can export your data anytime to create backups.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data. This creates a backup file that you can save to iCloud, Google Drive, or share with other devices.

What types of products can I track?

You can track any product with an expiry date: food items, medicines, vitamins, cosmetics, baby products, and any packaged goods.
